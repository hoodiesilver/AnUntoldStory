Our Goal

We as humans all possess natural inalienable rights simply by being human. Our goal is to educate you on your own human rights. Through this website, we will go through what the UDHR (Universal Declaration of Human Rights) is, what these rights mean, as well as how they are all relevant to us as humans.

In addition, An Untold Story will take a deeper look into the lives of refugees and immigrants, showing you a simplified version of how these people apply and go through the long and strenuous vetting process, a story that is not told enough.

Amnesty

Bio: Amnesty International is a global movement of more than 7 million people who take injustice personally. They are campaigning for a world where human rights are enjoyed by all. Through their detailed research and determined campaigning, Amnesty helps fight abuses of human rights worldwide by bringing torturers to justice, changing oppressive laws, and freeing people jailed just for voicing their opinion.

Unicef

Bio: UNICEF works in over 190 countries and territories to save children's lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfil their potential, from early childhood through adolescence. UNICEF works with partners around the world to promote policies and expand access to services that protect all children. They belive every child has the right to grow up in a safe and inclusive enviroment and the right to survive and thrive.

Human Rights Watch

Bio: Human Rights Watch investigates and reports on abuses happening in all corners of the world. They have about 450 people of 70-plus nationalities who are country experts, lawyers, journalists, and others who work to protect the most at risk, from vulnerable minorities and civilians in wartime, to refugees and children in need. They direct our advocacy towards governments and armed groups and businesses, pushing them to change or enforce their laws.

How can you help?

First, get educated about your own Human Rights. You can do this by looking through the rest of our website. Be sure to donate and support the organizations above! After that is done, here are a few ways to help promote human rights:

  1. Support Organizations that focus on Human Rights
  2. Spread the word: Inform your friends, family, and others about their own human rights!
  3. Send letters to your local administrators about issues you would like to address.
  4. Call out organizations or people that are violating human rights.
  5. Have open dialogue with your peers about human rights!
